**Click the GakrCLI icon in the side bar or editor toolbar to open a chat panel.**

You can also use the keyboard shortcut **Ctrl+Escape** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+Escape** (Mac) to quickly open or focus GakrCLI from anywhere in the editor.

To open in a dedicated tab, run **GakrCLI: Open in New Tab** from the Command Palette.
